## About Me

I am an **M.Sc. Computer Science** student at the **Central University of Rajasthan, Ajmer**, specializing in **deep learning** and **medical image analysis**. My research focuses on applying AI to solve real-world healthcare challenges, particularly in histopathological image analysis.

- **Research Intern** @ IIT Kharagpur — working on signal analysis and activity recognition
- **Research Intern** @ IIITDM Kurnool — histopathological image analysis for liver disease classification
- Actively seeking **PhD / Research positions** in AI for Healthcare and Computer Vision
